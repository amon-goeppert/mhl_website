Partnership Models

We provide five distinct partnership models tailored to the needs of our members. Our core mission is to facilitate access to exclusive, high-quality co-investment opportunities and to build a trusted global network.

Club Membership for LPs

An invitation-only community for family offices and UHNWIs. Members gain access to our global network, exclusive events, and curated deal flow.

Strategic Partnerships

We collaborate with selected fund managers (GPs) and organizations to share resources, deal flow, and market insights.

Co-Investment Opportunities

Advisory Services

Leveraging our deep industry expertise, we offer strategic advice to family offices on global investment strategies and to companies seeking to expand.

Future Plan - Fund of Funds

Our Network & Investment Focus

Our alliance is built on a foundation of trust with over 100 Single and Multi-Family Offices and UHNWIs from Europe and Asia. We focus on key investment themes driven by global trends.

Member Profiles:

  • Single Family Offices (SFOs): Often first or second-generation wealth from sectors like real estate, healthcare, and technology.
  • Multi-Family Offices (MFOs): Established firms managing wealth for multiple families.
  • Ultra-High-Net-Worth Individuals (UHNWIs): Successful entrepreneurs and industry leaders.
